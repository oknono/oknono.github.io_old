Today I accidentally booked a wrong tour, and went on a bike ride through the Cambodian countryside, instead of Angkor Wat. Oh well, it was lovely anyways, and quite hot, but Bangkok weather has hardened me somewhat. The abandoned temples in the forest reminded me of “The legend of Zelda”. Check out KKO Cambodia if you want to do a bike tour in Siem Reap. Good tours and good cause.

It is pretty nice to be out of the city. I might have to spend more time out of Bangkok next month. Little distraction so I can get more work done and have a bit more focus. Later this week I’ll be going to Phnom Penh and Kampot. The latter might be a good place to stay a bit longer. I thought I’d ideally want to spend my time between living in an Asian city (Bangkok!) and an European city (Berlin!), but maybe I’ll need to throw in some non-city life as well. Oh well, we’ll see.

Angkor Wat tomorrow!
